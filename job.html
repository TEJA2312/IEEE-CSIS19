IEEE CSIS with an aim to offer a platform for the students to meet, interact and exhibit their inner potentials, a job fair is being conducted as a part of CSIS since last year. The fair ended up selecting skilled students by recruiters from Unimity Solutions, Kolkata Ventures, Geomeo Informatics. This year CSIS’19 with a bigger motto is providing a wider opportunity for the graduating students to showcase their endowments. The final year graduates are directly recruited whereas the Second and third year graduates are offered an Internship program.
